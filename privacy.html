StayCool Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Overview

StayCool is a mobile application that helps users manage their wardrobe and discover outfits. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (email address) when you sign up
  • Clothing item photos you upload or scan
  • Wardrobe data you add to the app

Gmail Integration (Optional)

If you choose to connect Gmail, StayCool requests read-only access to search for clothing order confirmation emails from retailers. We use this solely to detect clothing items you've purchased and add them to your wardrobe.

  • We do not read, store, or share the content of your emails
  • We only scan for order confirmation emails from clothing retailers
  • You can disconnect Gmail access at any time
