StayCool

Your AI-powered wardrobe app. Scan clothing, discover outfits, and track your purchases — all in one place.

Features

  • Scan and organize clothing into a digital closet
  • Get AI-powered outfit suggestions
  • Import past purchases from Gmail order confirmations
  • Search and save products from the web

Privacy

When you connect Gmail, StayCool only reads order confirmation emails to detect clothing items. We never store or share your email content.
